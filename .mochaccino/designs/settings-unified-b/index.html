Design Notes — Variant B

  • Accordion over tabs: All classifier dimensions visible as sections rather than hidden behind tab selection. Scan the full picture at a glance, drill into any dimension.
  • Session Types always open: The primary 6-type breakdown is the most important view and stays expanded by default.
  • Delta tables appear after sync: Idle state shows simple count tables. Post-sync state shows before/after/delta columns with colour-coded changes.
  • Re-classify is secondary: Tucked behind a text link that expands an explanation + confirmation. Not a checkbox that might confuse — an explicit destructive action.
  • Stats strip always visible: Last sync timestamp, total session count, and classification coverage at a glance without scrolling.
  • Delta badges on collapsed sections: Even without opening an accordion, you can see +17 or no change badges indicating whether that dimension was affected by the sync.
  • Autonomy histogram: A small inline histogram for the continuous 0.0-1.0 autonomy ratio, more informative than a table for this data shape.

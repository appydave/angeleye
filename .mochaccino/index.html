Phase 1 — Theme Exploration 5 themes
2026-03-15

Five visual directions for the AngelEye observer. Each theme explores a different relationship between dark and light zones, card elevation, and typographic weight. v2-linen was selected as the design direction.

1
Paper
Warm parchment + dark sidebar

A well-designed document. Warm parchment as the main canvas, dark sidebar as the one intentional dark zone. Sessions feel like entries in an operations log.

Observer Organiser
2
Linen
Floating cards on warm linen canvas
Selected

Sessions float as white cards above a richer linen canvas. The card/background elevation is the design. Dark column header for structural weight.

Observer Organiser
3
Continuity
Dark nav + warm cream content

Dark sidebar as navigation home, warm cream as the content world. You live in the dark nav but work in the light.

Observer Organiser
4
Cockpit Light
Active sessions render dark — live instruments

Light canvas overall but active sessions render as dark rows. Like lit instruments on an operations panel. Dark = live, light = history.

Observer Organiser
5
Brief
Typography as the interface

Maximum typographic clarity. No cards, no chrome. Session names are the hero. An amber rule divides live from history.
